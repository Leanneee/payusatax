Payment Verification

Payment Verification allows you to verify prior payments processed through payUSAtax or 844-PAY-TAX-8. Our system will show you the payment date, confirmation number, tax type, amount paid, and the convenience fee for all payments made through our system for the prior 12 months. You do not have to be a registered user. Once you verify your prior payments, you will have the option to make additional payments through an expedited process.
